Welcome to New Journey Preschool

We offer a nurturing and joyful environment for early childhood education. At New Journey, learning is fun, engaging, and inspiring!

About Us

New Journey Preschool was founded to provide a safe and creative space for young minds to grow. Our mission is to build a strong educational foundation through play, exploration, and love. Our experienced staff ensures every child feels cared for and valued.

Our Programs

  • Nursery (2.5 - 3.5 years): Focus on sensory development, rhymes, basic vocabulary, group play.
  • Junior KG (3.5 - 4.5 years): Alphabets, numbers, colors, storytelling, drawing, pre-writing skills.
  • Senior KG (4.5 - 5.5 years): Reading, writing, math concepts, confidence building, social skills.
